Caesar Dressing Two Ways

Classic Caesar:

Ingredients:

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tsp. coarse salt
  • 1 tsp. dijon mustard
  • 1 1/2 tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp. vegan Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Roughly chop garlic and capers. Add coarse salt and chop/mash into a paste. Add to bowl
  2. Whisk rest of ingredients. Add salt/pepper as needed

Creamy Steakhouse Caesar:

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp. dry Ranch Dressing mix
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/4 cup Italian dressing
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Whisk all ingredients until thoroughly combined.
